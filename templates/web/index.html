About The Event

When Covid-19 struck in the middle of 2020, Several countries have adopted new adaptation strategies in fighting the pandemic in the year 2021, Many countries implement adaptation methods such as making vaccines available to their citizens through thorough distribution. Including indonesia, while Indonesia doesn't self-produce vaccines, the country proclaimed itself to be the fourth country to do mass vaccinations among its citizens. but Whether Indonesia and the international community are doing enough.

The incident above is part of the human security problem. "Human Security," freedom from fear, and the freedom of desires along with this concept were then added as the cornerstones of this concept. Consequently, we can recognize an important paradigm shift between "security" and "development". Individuals will now take center stage, demonstrating the previously state-centric nature of the previous approach. The key message from the International Conference is highlighted by this exhortation. As discussed above, Human Security is both development and protection, which encompasses widespread and non-military security concerns. based on the background Human Security will be the topic of the International Conference on "International Conference On Current Legal Issue And Human Security"
